Dayan
EMF 5ORBSB
STALKER

Behavior & Unique Ability

Hunt threshold/speed changes based on nearest player movement within 10m. Still = ~45% threshold, 1.2 m/s. Walking = ~65% threshold, 2.25 m/s.

Key Tools

Salt (Tier III) buys time. Sanity Medication T3 gives 10s sprint boost for escaping the danger zone.

How to Confirm

Keep one player still, another walking near ghost. Acceleration on movement + calm on stillness = Dayan.

Watch Out For

Any ghost speeds up via LoS — Dayan's tell is specifically movement-state within 10m.

💡 Tip

Use motion sensors to standardize the 10m distance — eyeballing it is unreliable.

Demon
WRTUVFRZ
AGGRESSOR

Behavior & Unique Ability

Can rarely hunt at any sanity. Incense prevention only lasts 60s (vs 90s normal). Crucifix range is +50% larger (4.5/6/7.5m by tier).

Key Tools

Crucifix is disproportionately valuable — place early and cover common hunt start points.

How to Confirm

Incense timer discipline: if you cleanse and get a hunt again before the expected 90s, Demon rises sharply. Post-crucifix delay only 20s vs 25s.

Watch Out For

Cursed hunts ignore incense prevention — don't attribute curse-chain hunts to Demon.

💡 Tip

Track sanity with the truck monitor rather than guessing. Demon's "any sanity" hunts are rare, not constant.

Deogen
DOTSWRTSB
SPECIALIZED

Behavior & Unique Ability

Always knows where you are during hunts — hiding doesn't work. Very fast approach but becomes extremely slow up close.

How to Confirm

During a hunt, if it hard-tracks you but slows dramatically at close range, that's the Deogen signature.

Watch Out For

Don't confuse with Moroi/Revenant speed spikes — Deogen's "slow close-range" is unique.

💡 Tip

You CAN outrun a Deogen at close range — loop around furniture. The danger is the approach speed.

Gallu
EMF 5UVSB
AGGRESSOR

Behavior & Unique Ability

Becomes aggressive when protective equipment is used; calms after hunts. State-dependent salt behavior and crucifix range changes.

Key Tools

Salt: enraged Gallus ignore salt (like Wraiths). Crucifix range: −2m enraged, +1m weakened. Incense forces state change.

How to Confirm

Salt choke test + protection tool state changes. Wraith always ignores salt; Gallu only when enraged — use protection to trigger state and compare.

Watch Out For

Salt avoidance can be pathing RNG — use doorway choke points with long Tier II/III lines.

💡 Tip

Gallu punishes you for using safety tools carelessly. Plan your protection gear usage deliberately.

Pro Tips & Tricks

Electronics Kill

Active electronics within 7.5m attract the ghost during hunts. Turn everything off when hiding. Yokai only has 2.5m photo camera attraction.

EMF 5 = 33% Chance

Each EMF 2–3 event only has 33% chance to show EMF 5 for evidence ghosts. Check interactions fast and often — don't rule it out early.

Freezing Threshold

Non-freezing ghosts bottom out at ~1°C. Freezing ghosts reach −10°C. Confirm only below 1°C. Tier I: wait for below 0°C due to ±3°C fluctuation.

The Mimic's Orb Trick

Mimic ALWAYS shows Ghost Orbs as extra false evidence, even at 0 evidence settings. Orbs + SB + Freezing + UV = strongly suspect Mimic.

Incense Timing

Standard no-hunt: 90s. Spirit: 180s. Demon: 60s. Time your incense — this is one of the most reliable behavioral confirms in the game.

Salt Strategy

Don't scatter randomly. Create unavoidable lines in doorways with Tier II/III. Confirm traversal with motion sensors. Pristine salt = Wraith or enraged Gallu.

UV Print Window

Prints last 2 min (1 min Insanity). After hunts, immediately scan all doors and switches — hunts create many door touches. Speed is critical.

D.O.T.S Camera Trick

Goryo only shows D.O.T.S on camera, not in-person. Always set up video camera watching projector and check from truck. Night vision helps dramatically.

Sanity Math

Know med restore by difficulty (40/35/30/25/20%). Track via truck monitor. Lets you determine if hunts are "early" — key for Demon, Shade, Yokai identification.
